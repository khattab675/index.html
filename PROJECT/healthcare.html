About Us









Medical Clinic

Our on-campus medical clinic provides comprehensive healthcare services to students, including general check-ups, vaccinations, and treatment for common illnesses. Our professional staff ensures a high standard of care and confidentiality.

Mental Health Services

The Mental Health Services team offers support for students facing emotional or mental health challenges. Services include counseling, therapy sessions, and wellness workshops to promote mental well-being and resilience.

Nutrition Counseling

Our nutrition counseling services provide students with personalized dietary advice to improve their health and well-being. Our nutritionists offer consultations on balanced diets, healthy eating habits, and specialized dietary needs.

On-Campus Pharmacy

The on-campus pharmacy offers a wide range of over-the-counter and prescription medications. Our pharmacists are available to advise on medication safety, dosage, and potential side effects.

Fitness and Wellness Programs

We provide various fitness and wellness programs, including yoga, meditation, and stress management workshops. These programs are designed to help students maintain physical health and cope with academic stress.
