About Computer Engineering

Computer Engineering (CE) combines elements of both electrical engineering and computer science to create a unique and versatile discipline. It focuses on the design, development, and application of computer systems and hardware, along with the software that drives them. As technology continues to evolve, computer engineers play a critical role in innovations in fields such as robotics, artificial intelligence, and data science.

Core Subjects in Computer Engineering

  • Digital Logic Design
  • Microprocessor and Microcontroller Systems
  • Computer Networks and Communications
  • Operating Systems
  • Software Engineering
  • Embedded Systems
  • Database Management Systems
  • Algorithms and Data Structures
  • Computer Architecture
  • Cybersecurity

Career Opportunities

A degree in Computer Engineering opens up a wide range of career opportunities, including:

  • Computer Engineer
  • Embedded Systems Engineer
  • Network Engineer
  • Software Developer
  • Hardware Design Engineer
  • Systems Architect
  • Cybersecurity Analyst
  • Data Scientist
  • AI/ML Engineer
  • Robotics Engineer
