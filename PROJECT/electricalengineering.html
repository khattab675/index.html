About Electrical Engineering

Electrical Engineering (EE) focuses on the study, design, and application of systems that generate, control, and utilize electrical power. It is one of the most diverse and rapidly evolving engineering fields, integrating knowledge from physics, mathematics, and computer science.

Core Subjects in Electrical Engineering

  • Circuit Theory and Design
  • Digital Signal Processing
  • Power Systems and Power Electronics
  • Microelectronics and Semiconductor Devices
  • Control Systems and Automation
  • Electromagnetic Fields and Waves
  • Communication Systems
  • Renewable Energy Technologies

Career Opportunities

Graduates with a degree in Electrical Engineering have a wide range of career paths available to them, including but not limited to:

  • Electrical Engineer
  • Power Systems Engineer
  • Control Systems Engineer
  • Electronics Design Engineer
  • Communication Systems Engineer
  • Renewable Energy Consultant
  • Automation and Robotics Engineer
  • R&D Engineer in Semiconductor Industry
