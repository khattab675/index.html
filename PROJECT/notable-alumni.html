Notable Alumni

Cairo University is proud of its distinguished alumni, including three Nobel laureates:

  • Naguib Mahfouz: Nobel Prize in Literature (1988)
  • Ahmed Zewail: Nobel Prize in Chemistry (1999)
  • Mohamed ElBaradei: Nobel Peace Prize (2005)

Our alumni have gone on to make significant contributions in various fields, including politics, arts, sciences, and business. They embody the university's tradition of excellence and service.
