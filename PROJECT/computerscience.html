About Us









Overview

The Computer Science major prepares students for a wide range of careers in the tech industry, focusing on the principles of computation, programming, algorithms, and data analysis. The field offers endless opportunities for creativity, problem-solving, and innovation.

Skills Acquired

  • Programming in multiple languages (e.g., Python, Java, C++)
  • Software development and engineering
  • Problem-solving and algorithm design
  • Data analysis and machine learning
  • Cybersecurity and ethical hacking
  • Database design and management
  • Systems and network administration

Career Opportunities

A degree in Computer Science opens doors to many exciting careers, such as:

Software Developer

Design and create software applications for various platforms.

Data Scientist

Analyze large sets of data to extract insights and drive decision-making.

Cybersecurity Specialist

Protect organizations from cyber threats and ensure data security.

Systems Administrator

Manage and maintain computer systems and networks within an organization.

Relevant Courses

  • Introduction to Programming
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Operating Systems
  • Database Systems
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Machine Learning
  • Computer Networks
  • Software Engineering
