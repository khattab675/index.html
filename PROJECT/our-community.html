About Us









Our Community

With approximately 155,000 students and a dedicated faculty, Cairo University is a bustling academic community. Our students come from diverse backgrounds, united by a shared passion for learning and discovery. Our faculty members are leaders in their fields, committed to teaching and mentoring the next generation of scholars.

The university offers a vibrant campus life, with numerous student organizations, clubs, and activities that foster personal growth and community engagement. Our state-of-the-art facilities provide students with the resources they need to succeed.
