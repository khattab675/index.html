Admission Requirements for International Baccalaureate (IB) Students

Welcome to [University Name]! We are thrilled to welcome students with the International Baccalaureate (IB) diploma to our campus. Below are the requirements for IB students applying for undergraduate programs. Be sure to review this information carefully before beginning your application process.

General Admission Requirements

  • Secondary Education: Completion of the International Baccalaureate (IB) Diploma or an equivalent secondary school qualification recognized by the university.
  • Minimum GPA: A minimum overall score of 24 points in the IB Diploma, or an equivalent score based on your country's grading system.
  • English Proficiency: If your IB diploma was completed in English, you may not need additional proof of English proficiency. However, if your IB diploma was not completed in English, you will need to demonstrate English proficiency through one of the following methods:
    • TOEFL: Minimum score of 80 (iBT) or 550 (PBT).
    • IELTS: Minimum score of 6.0 to 6.5 (depending on the program).
    • Cambridge English Exams: Minimum C1 Advanced level (CAE) or Proficiency level (CPE).
    • English-Medium Education: If you completed the IB diploma in an English-speaking institution, you may be exempt from this requirement.

Program-Specific Requirements

In addition to the general admission requirements, each academic program may have specific requirements. Below are some common guidelines for IB students:

  • STEM Programs: A strong background in mathematics and science is required. Higher Level (HL) subjects in Mathematics and Science are preferred. A grade of 5 or higher in these subjects is recommended.
  • Business & Economics: A background in Mathematics is recommended, and Higher Level (HL) Mathematics is preferred. Some programs may also require standardized test scores (e.g., SAT, ACT).
  • Creative Arts and Humanities: For certain programs such as Fine Arts, Architecture, or Journalism, portfolios, writing samples, or other creative submissions may be required.

Application Documents

To apply, you will need to submit the following documents:

  • Completed Application Form: Submit your online application form through our admissions portal.
  • Official IB Transcripts: Provide your official IB transcript, along with a certified English translation (if applicable).
  • Proof of English Proficiency: Submit your TOEFL, IELTS, or other accepted test scores, or proof of completing the IB diploma in English.
  • Recommendation Letters: Two or more letters of recommendation from teachers or other academic professionals who are familiar with your academic performance.
  • Passport Copy: A copy of your valid passport for identification purposes.
  • Financial Documents: Proof of financial support to cover tuition and living expenses during your studies (e.g., bank statements, affidavit of support).

Important Notes:

  • Application Deadline: Please ensure you submit your application at least 6 months prior to the start of the academic year.
  • Visa Requirements: All international students will need to obtain a student visa for study in [Country]. Our admissions team will assist with this process once your application is accepted.
