About Us









Gymnasium

Our on-campus gymnasium is fully equipped with modern fitness equipment, providing students with a space for strength training, cardio workouts, and group exercise classes.

Swimming Pool

The university offers a large indoor swimming pool, accessible year-round for both recreational swimming and swimming lessons. Lifeguards are always on duty to ensure safety.

Sports Fields

We provide well-maintained fields for outdoor sports, including football, soccer, and rugby. Students can join organized teams or play casually with friends.

Tennis Courts

The university has multiple tennis courts available for student use. These courts are open for practice sessions, tournaments, and casual play.

Basketball Courts

Our outdoor and indoor basketball courts are available for both competitive and recreational games. Join our basketball club or play casually with fellow students.
