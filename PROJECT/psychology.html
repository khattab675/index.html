About Psychology

Psychology is the scientific study of the mind and behavior. As a psychology major, you will explore various aspects of human thought, emotion, and behavior, examining how individuals perceive, interact with, and respond to their environments. The field integrates knowledge from biology, sociology, and cognitive science, preparing you to understand and address complex psychological phenomena.

Core Subjects in Psychology

  • Introduction to Psychology
  • Cognitive Psychology
  • Abnormal Psychology
  • Developmental Psychology
  • Social Psychology
  • Psychological Research Methods
  • Neuroscience and Behavior
  • Personality Psychology
  • Health Psychology
  • Psychological Assessment and Testing

Career Opportunities

Graduates with a degree in Psychology can pursue a wide range of careers in various settings, such as healthcare, education, business, and social services. Some career paths include:

  • Clinical Psychologist
  • Counseling Psychologist
  • Industrial-Organizational Psychologist
  • Forensic Psychologist
  • School Psychologist
  • Researcher or Academic
  • Human Resources Specialist
  • Health Educator
  • Substance Abuse Counselor
  • Market Research Analyst
