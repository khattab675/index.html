About Us









Career Guidance

Our Career Guidance program helps students explore career options based on their interests, strengths, and academic backgrounds. We provide individual counseling sessions to support students in making informed career choices.

Resume Workshops

Our Resume Workshops provide students with guidance on building effective and professional resumes. Students learn how to highlight their skills, experiences, and achievements to make a strong impression on potential employers.

Internship Opportunities

We connect students with internships across various fields, providing practical experience and networking opportunities. Our team collaborates with companies to secure internship positions that align with studentsâ€™ career goals.

Interview Preparation

The Interview Preparation service helps students build confidence and communication skills. We offer mock interviews, feedback, and tips on answering common questions to help students excel in real interviews.

Career Fairs

Our annual career fairs bring a variety of companies and organizations to campus, offering students the chance to connect with potential employers, learn about job openings, and discover career paths in various industries.
